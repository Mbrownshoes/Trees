Toronto tree map



About tree data

Most of the data comes from the city of Toronto's tree database. Tree tree locations are not entirely accurate, so users are encouraged to move the trees to their correct location using the editor. The Harbord village Residents' association tree inventory is also inluded.

Contact

Feel free to email me to provide feedback or to let me know of any other databases I can include.
